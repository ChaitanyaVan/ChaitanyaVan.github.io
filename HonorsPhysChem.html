Unit One: Atomic Structure

The atom consists of the nucleus,neutrons, protons, electrons, and orbitals.The nucleus contains
protons and neutrons. Protons have a positive charge and neutrons have a neutral
charge while electrons have a negative charge.

Unit Two:Bonding

There are two different kinds of bonds. Ionic and Covalent.
Covalent is the sharing of electrons while Ionic is the "stealing" of electrons.
There are two different types of covalent bonds. Polar and Non-Polar depending the difference of electrong negativity.
